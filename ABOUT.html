ABOUT STEVEN MANES

Steven Antonio J Manes, born in 1993, studies Sculpture in Ghent. Artist of Italian descent who makes abstract sculptures and drawings about his experience of living in this perishable world in which we all have to live. Strong feelings are being translated into images. Spontaneous and abstract. Searching for relief and understanding.
